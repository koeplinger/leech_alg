conditions} is a rank-24 Z-module embedded inside L³ where L = E8 = integral
octonions.  Because L carries the octonion product, the question of whether an
octonion-based product on R^24 closes on Λ is well-posed — Λ is naturally the
ambient object for such an investigation, and this is how the community frames
the question (see also Dixon [Dixon2010]).
