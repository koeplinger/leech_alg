## Naming Convention

Files should be named descriptively, using lowercase with underscores. Where a document has a canonical identifier (e.g., arXiv ID, DOI), include it in the filename. Examples:

- `lastname_year_short_title.pdf`
- `arxiv_XXXX_XXXXX_short_title.pdf`

## Usage

When a document is cited in the research, it must also be registered in [../evidence_and_reasoning/references/](../evidence_and_reasoning/references/) with full bibliographic information and topic tags.

## Important Notes

- Only documents that are freely redistributable are held in this folder. Publisher-copyright PDFs and arXiv preprints under the nonexclusive-distrib license are listed in "Externally hosted sources" above and are not committed to the repository.
- Do not include any documents containing personal or sensitive information.
